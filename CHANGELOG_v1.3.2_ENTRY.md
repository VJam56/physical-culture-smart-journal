### Added

- ADR-008: Protect the Athlete's Concentration

### Clarified

- Every primary interaction must reduce cognitive burden or preserve coaching-quality evidence.
- Capture occurs during the training event; organization and analysis may occur afterward.
- The active objective must visually dominate the session screen.
- Record keeping must not compete with lifting execution.
