# Readiness Engine

## MVP Approach

Begin with transparent rules and comparisons before machine learning.

Inputs may include:

- Sleep duration
- Sleep quality
- Stress
- Energy
- Motivation
- Soreness
- Recent training load
- Performance deviation
- Nutrition deviation
- Supplement deviation

## Output

A contextual readiness summary with:

- Current inputs
- Difference from personal baseline
- Relevant recent history
- Uncertainty
- No automatic training prescription
