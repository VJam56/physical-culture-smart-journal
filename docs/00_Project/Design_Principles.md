# Design Principles

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Approved

1. Reduce friction.
2. Prepare the workspace before training begins.
3. Preserve immediate observations at the point of action.
4. Link every entry to its correct context.
5. Let AI organize evidence, not overrule the human.
6. Observe rather than diagnose.
7. Preserve user agency.
8. Keep the interface uncluttered.
9. Make all stored data analytically meaningful.
10. Prefer longitudinal evidence over isolated readings.
11. Protect privacy by design.
12. Lock the MVP until real-world usage reveals what is missing.
